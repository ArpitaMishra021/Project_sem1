Agra

About Agra

Agra is home to the world-famous Taj Mahal and is known for its rich Mughal heritage. The city boasts historic forts, palaces, and bustling markets.

Top Attractions

  • Taj Mahal – Iconic Monument
  • Agra Fort – Historical Fortress
  • Fatehpur Sikri – Ancient City
  • Itimad-ud-Daulah – Baby Taj
  • Mehtab Bagh – Garden with Taj View

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Agra is from October to March when the weather is cool and comfortable for sightseeing.
