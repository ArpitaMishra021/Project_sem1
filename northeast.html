Northeast India

About Northeast India

Northeast India is a region of scenic beauty, rich culture, and diverse tribes. It is known for lush hills, rivers, waterfalls, and adventure tourism.

Top Attractions

  • Shillong – Scotland of the East
  • Gangtok – Scenic Hill Station
  • Kaziranga National Park – Wildlife Safari
  • Tawang – Monasteries & Mountains
  • Majuli – River Island in Brahmaputra

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Northeast India is from October to April when the weather is pleasant for sightseeing and trekking.
