Northeast India

About Northeast India

Northeast India is a region of scenic beauty, rich culture, and diverse tribes. It is known for lush hills, rivers, waterfalls, and adventure tourism.

Top Attractions

  • Shillong – Scotland of the East
  • Gangtok – Scenic Hill Station
  • Kaziranga National Park – Wildlife Safari
  • Tawang – Monasteries & Mountains
  • Majuli – River Island in Brahmaputra

Adventures & Outdoors

  • Kaziranga National Park (Assam): Home to the Great One-horned Rhinoceros; also explore Nameri.
  • Meghalaya: See stunning waterfalls (Nohkalikai, Elephant Falls) and the famous living root bridges.
  • Ziro Valley (Arunachal Pradesh): UNESCO site known for natural beauty and tribal culture
  • Tawang Monastery (Arunachal Pradesh): One of the largest monasteries, at high altitude.
  • Khonoma Village (Nagaland): Historic village known for resisting British rule.
  • Hornbill Festival (Nagaland): Experience diverse Naga tribal culture.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Northeast India is from October to April when the weather is pleasant for sightseeing and trekking.
