Rajasthan

About Rajasthan

Rajasthan is known as the Land of Kings. It is famous for its royal palaces, historic forts, colorful culture, and vast deserts. The state reflects a rich blend of tradition and heritage.

Top Attractions

  • Jaipur – The Pink City
  • Udaipur – City of Lakes
  • Jaisalmer – Desert Safari
  • Jodhpur – Mehrangarh Fort
  • Pushkar – Holy City

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Rajasthan is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and suitable for sightseeing.
