Himachal

About Himachal

Himachal Pradesh is a beautiful state located in the northern part of India, surrounded by the majestic Himalayas. It is famous for its snow-covered mountains, lush green valleys, rivers, and pleasant climate. The state is often called “Dev Bhoomi”, which means Land of the Gods, because of its many ancient temples and spiritual sites.

Top Attractions

  • Shimla - The capital city, known for its colonial architecture and cool weather
  • Manali - Famous for snow, adventure sports, and scenic views
  • Dharamshala - Home to the Dalai Lama and Tibetan culture
  • Dalhousie - A quiet hill station with beautiful landscapes
  • Spiti Valley - Known for its cold desert and monasteries

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Himachal Pradesh is March to June for pleasant weather and sightseeing. For snow lovers, December to February is ideal to enjoy snowfall and winter sports.
