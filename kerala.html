Kerala

About Kerala

Kerala, the God's Own Country, is famous for its backwaters, lush greenery, beaches, and unique culture. The state is known for Ayurveda, houseboats, and vibrant festivals.

Top Attractions

  • Alleppey – Backwater Cruises
  • Munnar – Tea Plantations
  • Kochi – Fort Kochi & Chinese Fishing Nets
  • Wayanad – Wildlife & Waterfalls
  • Varkala – Cliff-side Beaches

Outdoor Activities

  • Houseboat Stays: Overnight or day cruises through Alleppey (Alappuzha) or Kumarakom's serene canals.
  • Wildlife & Forests: Explore Periyar National Park for safaris and bamboo rafting, or visit Wayanad for jungles and camping.
  • Tea Plantations: Tour tea estates in Munnar, often including jeep safaris and tasting.
  • Jatayu Earth Center: Visit the massive bird sculpture in Chadayamangalam, reachable by cable car.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kerala is from September to March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing.
