Kashmir

About Kashmir

Kashmir, known as Paradise on Earth, is famous for its stunning landscapes, serene valleys, and beautiful gardens. The region offers snow-capped mountains, pristine lakes, and rich culture.

Top Attractions

  • Srinagar - Dal Lake and Houseboats
  • Gulmarg - Skiing and Adventure
  • Doodhpathri- Valley of Milk, a picturesque meadow destination.
  • Sonamarg - Meadow of Gold
  • Yusmarg - Tranquil Nature

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kashmir is from April to October for sightseeing and from December to February for snow activities.
